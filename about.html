About Napell BIO

We're Not Just
Growing Coffee

We're redefining what's possible when agriculture meets data science. Napell BIO is the company behind the world's first commercial aeroponic coffee system — and we're just getting started.

Our Mission

Climate-Independent
Coffee for Every City

Traditional coffee cultivation is approaching a wall. Climate change, water scarcity, and deforestation are making conventional arabica production increasingly unviable. Yet demand continues to rise.

Napell BIO exists to break that dependency. By moving coffee production into controlled urban environments using precision aeroponic systems, we can grow premium arabica anywhere on Earth — with 92% less water, zero pesticides, and a 10,000km → 10km supply chain.

Our pilot farm in Nanshan, Shenzhen proves the concept. Now we're scaling.

What Drives Us

The principles that guide every decision we make — from sensor calibration to talent hiring.

Data Over Opinion

We don't guess. Every environmental parameter is measured, every yield prediction is backed by sensor data, and every quality claim is verified by independent Q-graders.

Open Ecosystem

We believe the future of agriculture is collaborative. Our APIs are open, our hardware specs are shared, and our data is available to researchers and partners.

Relentless Innovation

Aeroponics is just the beginning. We're constantly optimizing — from LED spectrum tuning to root-zone oxygen management to AI-driven flavor profiling.

Circular by Design

Every drop of water and every gram of nutrient is accounted for. Our closed-loop system recovers 99% of nutrients. Waste is a design flaw, not a necessity.
