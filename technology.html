The Science of
Aeroponic Coffee

We've replaced soil with a precision nutrient fog, delivered directly to the root zone at microsecond intervals. Here's how it works.

01 — Aeroponic Delivery

Nutrient Fog, Not Soil

In a traditional system, roots fight for oxygen. In our aeroponic towers, roots hang in a dark, oxygen-rich chamber where high-pressure mist heads deliver a micron-scale nutrient fog every 2.5 minutes.

This means 100% oxygen availability at the root zone — something soil or even deep-water culture can never achieve. The result: 3× faster growth and significantly higher brix (sugar content) in the cherry.
